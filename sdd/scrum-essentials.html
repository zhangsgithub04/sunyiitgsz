Scrum

Agile Framework for Software Development

From Rugby to Software: A framework emphasizing teamwork, collaboration, and iterative progress

Etymology: "Scrum" comes from rugby's "scrummage" - a tight formation to restart play.

Origin: Introduced by Hirotaka Takeuchi & Ikujiro Nonaka (Harvard Business Review, 1986)
